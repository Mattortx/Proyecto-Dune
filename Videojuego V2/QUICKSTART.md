# 🎮 Quick Start Guide - Casa Portil Game

## How to Play (TL;DR)

1. **Open `index.html`** in your browser
2. **Click "Start Game"**
3. **Use WASD/Arrows** to move
4. **Press SPACE** to talk to NPCs
5. **Press Q** to see quests

## What to Do

✅ **Talk to NPCs** - They have quests for you (look for yellow "!")
✅ **Find blue stations** - Measure water for rewards
✅ **Avoid red enemies** - They attack and steal water
✅ **Trade with merchant** - Exchange water ↔ credits
✅ **Complete quests** - Earn points and resources

## HUD Elements

- 💧 **Water** = Your life supply (don't let it hit 0!)
- ⚡ **Credits** = Money for trading
- 🏆 **Score** = Achievement points
- 📍 **Location** = Where you are

## Key NPCs

| NPC | Color | Quest Type |
|-----|-------|------------|
| Archivist Vanya | 🟣 Purple | Measure water stations |
| Captain Cael | 🔴 Red | Protect convoys |
| Merchant Seris | 🟢 Green | Trading |
| Priestess Ilyna | 🟡 Gold | Sacred ceremony quests |

## Tips

- Explore first, fight later
- Water is more valuable than credits
- Check minimap (bottom-right) for navigation
- Sandstorms slow you down
- Press ESC to pause

## Controls

```
Movement: WASD or Arrow Keys
Interact: SPACE
Quests: Q
Pause: ESC
```
